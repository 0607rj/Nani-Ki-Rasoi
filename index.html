Who We Are

Your Home Away From Home

Welcome to Nani Ki Rasoi! We are a mess that specializes in providing nutritious, affordable, and homely food for students who are studying away from their families. At Nani Ki Rasoi, we believe in the warmth of home-cooked meals and aim to bring that same comfort to your table every day.

Our mission is simple: to provide meals that make you feel like you're eating at your grandmother’s place — hearty, healthy, and prepared with love. Whether it's a delicious dal, comforting sabzi, or freshly made roti, our food is designed to remind you of home.

Our Core Values

  • Homely Taste: We make food just like your grandmother would — full of love and care.
  • Affordable Prices: Quality food doesn't have to be expensive. We keep our prices student-friendly!
  • Fresh & Nutritious: We focus on using fresh ingredients to ensure your meals are both healthy and tasty.
  • Warmth & Comfort: More than just food, we aim to create a place that feels like home for students away from family.
